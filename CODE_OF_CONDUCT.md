# Code of Conduct

PermeantOS aims to be a rigorous and welcoming research project. Contributors are expected to communicate with care, assume good intent, and keep technical disagreement constructive.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and specific in feedback.
- Welcome new contributors and basic questions.
- Prefer reproducible evidence over status or authority.
- Credit prior work and related systems fairly.
- Treat security-sensitive reports responsibly.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Publishing private user data, credentials, or sensitive migration artifacts.
- Deliberately disruptive conduct in issues, discussions, or pull requests.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate, edit, or remove comments and contributions that violate this code. Serious or repeated violations may lead to a ban from project spaces.
